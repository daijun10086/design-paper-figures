1. Asynchronous capture: blurred 1280×720 RGB frames plus raw 1280×720 event streams, without hardware synchronization.
  2. Event bridge: E2VID reconstruction, bilateral denoising, and multi-frame brightness balancing produce sharp grayscale frames.
  3. Cross-domain initialization: VGGT jointly processes RGB and event-derived frames to estimate all poses and a dense confidence-filtered point cloud.
  4. Two-stage optimization: Stage 1 trains an RGB-only coarse color reference; Stage 2 jointly refines 3DGS and poses with Lblur, Lreg-r, Levs, Lstruct, and Lreg-e.
  5. Output: an optimized, color-faithful 3DGS with sharp novel views; real input, structure-map, confidence-map, and output examples remain to be inserted.
